We are proud to announce our Vice Principal, Mr John Ow as a recipient of the Public Administration Medal (Bronze) or Pingat Pentadbiran Awam (Gangsa) this year. This medal is awarded to a person who has distinguished him or herself through commendable performance and conduct, or significant efficiency, competence and devotion to duty in the Ministry of Education amongst other governmental bodies.

#### **Caring Teacher Awards**
The Caring Teacher Awards (CTA) organized by NIE, supported by MOE and ExxonMobil Asia Pacific Pte Ltd is a biennial event. It pays tribute to teachers who show care and concern for the holistic development of their students. It recognizes the efforts of those who go the extra mile to ensure their charges grow up to be confident and independent learners in school and outside the classroom.  We are proud to have our staff achieving the Caring Teacher Award 2022 and thank them for their earnest contribution to the Horizon Family.

#### **MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA) 2021**
The MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA) recognises MOE staff who are passionate in their service delivery, consistently serve internal and external customers with C.A.R.E. (Courtesy, Accessibility, Responsiveness and Effectiveness), and inspire their colleagues to do likewise.

This year we are proud to recognize seven members of our Horizon Family as MOE Ambassadors who have been awarded the MSEA 2021.

They are Mdm Nurasyiqin Binte Mohamed Shafei (Platinum Award), Mdm Nurasyiqin Binte Mohamed Shafei (Gold Award), Mdm Fung Siew Koon (Gold Award), Mr Bay Ah Hock (Gold Award), Mr Yeo Soon Seng Gary (Silver Award), Mdm Ow Kum Chee Jennifer (Silver Award) and Mdm Chua Lian Hoong (Silver Award).  

Our heartiest congratulations to them and join me in sharing the joy as one Horizon Family.

We are very proud to have our Year Head Ms Shirley Lim to have received the **LEAP Commendation Award** and being a constant inspiration to our colleagues
