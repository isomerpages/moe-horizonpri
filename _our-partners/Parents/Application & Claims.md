<h3><strong>Parent's Gateway Mobile Application</strong></h3>
<p>Parents Gateway is one of our school's official communication platforms.</p>
<p>Please download the application in your mobile device by following the
step-by-step guide below.</p>
<p>1. Open the Apple App Store or Google Play Store app on your mobile phone.
<br>2. Search for the 'Parents Gateway' mobile app, or scan the QR code below.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:65%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/PG.jpg">
</div>
<p>3. Download and install the app on your phone.
<br>4. Enable '<strong>Allow Notifications</strong>' to receive push notifications.
<br>5. Log in the app with your SingPass. (If you have not yet registered
for a SingPass or have not yet set up the 2FA, please visit the SingPass
website (<a href="https://www.singpass.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.singpass.gov.sg</a>)
to do so.</p>
