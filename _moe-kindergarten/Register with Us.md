<h3><strong>Register for MOE Kindergarten @ Horizon</strong></h3>
<p><strong><u>OA Registration</u></strong>
</p>
<p>OA registration for 2027 K1 admissions is open to SC and PR children who
are:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Born between 2 January 2022 and
1 January 2023 (both dates inclusive); and are</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Either not enrolled in any partner
EYCs, or currently enrolled in an EYC but choose to register in a non-partner
MK.</p>
<p>Places for children registering via OA will be allocated based on the
<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/register/priority-admission" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">admission priority order</a>.</p>
<p><strong><u>Online registration</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Online Open Admissions (OA) and Early Years Centre (EYC) registration
for 2027 K1 admissions will open from 9am on Monday, 23 March 2026 and
close at 4.30 pm on Tuesday, 31 March 2026.</p>
<p><strong><u>Registration outcome</u></strong>
</p>
<p>You will be notified of the registration outcome via email by Monday,
1 June 2026.</p>
<p><strong><u>Waiting List</u></strong>
</p>
<p><u>Late Applications for MK@Horizon</u>
</p>
<p>If you wish to apply to our kindergarten after the official online registration
period has closed, please complete the application form using the link
provided below.</p>
<p><u>Important Information:</u>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Priority placement will be given
to all applications received during the official registration period</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Late applications will only be considered
if places remain available after the initial allocation</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; If no places are available, your
application will be added to our waiting list and we will contact you should
a vacancy arise</p>
<p><u>Specific Situations:</u>
</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 2027 K2 Admission: Parents seeking
direct entry to K2 level</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Missed K1 Registration: Parents
who were unable to submit applications during the general K1 registration
exercise</p>
<p>For both situations above, please use the provided link to complete your
application. We will reach out to you directly if places become available.</p>
<p>Link: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/2026mkhorizonwaitlistform" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MK@Horizon Waitlist</a>
</p>
