### **Clubs and Societies**
Through our Clubs and Societies CCAs, students get to explore and extend their interests in wide-ranging and specialised areas which may be knowledge-based or skills-based. Beginning with the end in mind, students are honed in information, communication and technical skills as they strive to grow their mastery of the specialised areas through these CCAs.
