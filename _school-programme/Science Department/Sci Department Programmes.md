<h4><strong>Excellence 2000 Science Programme</strong></h4>
<p>The Excellence 2000 (E2K) Science Programme is implemented at Horizon
Primary School to provide high-ability students with opportunities to engage
in deep scientific inquiry. This enrichment programme challenges students
to think and work like scientists, developing their critical questioning,
problem-solving skills, and ability to generate knowledge through hands-on
experimentation. Aligned with the core value of Excellence, the programme
nurtures curiosity and inspires students to excel in understanding and
exploring advanced scientific concepts​.</p>
<p>The E2K Science Programme emphasises resilience and resourcefulness as
students navigate authentic challenges in science. Recognising that mistakes
are part of the learning process, participants are encouraged to persevere
and reflect on their approaches, cultivating Integrity in their pursuit
of solutions. Through collaborative investigations and discussions, students
practice Compassion by supporting and learning from their peers. These
skills prepare them to face future challenges with confidence and adaptability​.</p>
<p>Through the structured activities and guided learning experiences of the
E2K Science Programme, students develop essential 21st-century competencies,
including critical and inventive thinking, effective communication, and
teamwork. The programme fosters a Respect for diverse perspectives and
encourages students to share their findings with clarity and confidence.</p>
