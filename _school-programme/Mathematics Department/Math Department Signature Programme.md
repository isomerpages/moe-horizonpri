<h4><strong>Young Mathematician Programme</strong></h4>
<p>At Horizon, we believe that every student has the potential to think,
speak, and act like a mathematician! Our <strong>Young Mathematician Programme</strong> takes
learning beyond the classroom, offering engaging, age-appropriate activities
that spark curiosity and build confidence in Math.</p>
<p>Through hands-on problem-solving, students apply Math concepts and critical
thinking skills while embracing challenges, learning from mistakes, and
refining their thought processesâ€”enhancing their metacognitive abilities.</p>
<p>This programme also empowers our <strong>Math Captains</strong>, providing
them with leadership opportunities to plan, conduct, and review recess
activities, fostering a vibrant and collaborative Math community in our
school.</p>
<p>Join us in discovering the joy of Mathematics in action!</p>
