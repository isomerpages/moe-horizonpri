<h3><strong>Trips for Internationalisation Experience (TIE) @ Horizon</strong></h3>
<p>Our students had the exciting opportunity to embark on overseas learning
journeys to Fujian Xiamen Experimental Primary School in China and Sekolah
Rendah Tumpuan Telisai in Brunei Darussalam. These trips provided authentic
experiences beyond the classroom, where students interacted with peers
from different countries, embraced diverse cultures, and gained valuable
insights into global perspectives.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Back in Singapore, Horizon Primary proudly hosted The Salvation Army Lam
Butt Chung Memorial School and Yan Chai Hospital Law Chan Chor Si Primary
School from Hong Kong, as well as Fujian Xiamen Experimental Primary School
from China. These exchanges sparked curiosity, built friendships, and fostered
cross-cultural understanding. They also strengthened studentsâ€™ 21st Century
Competencies and inspired them to grow as socially responsible, globally
competent leaders of tomorrow.</p>
